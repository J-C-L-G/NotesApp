Welcome to WebNotes.

Simple web application to create and store notes in your browser!

Live Demo!

Controls

Creating Notes

  1. Click on the "Create Note" button and provide a title and message.

Deleting Notes

  1. Drag the Note that you want to delete and drop it into the trashcan.

Search for content

  1. Write what you are searching for and click "Search".
  2. Once done clean the search field and click "Search" again to bring all the notes again.

Re-Arranging the Notes

  1. Drag and Drop, Simple!.
